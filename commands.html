🐟 Mossy Commands

Everything Mossy can currently do!

💬 Basic Commands

!hello - Mossy says hello!

!moss - Mossy reacts based on his current mood.

!think - Random fishy thoughts.

!mood - Shows Mossy's current mood.

!changemood - Gives Mossy a new mood.

🫧 Fun Commands

!bubble - Creates bubbles.

!feedmossy - Feed Mossy fish food.

!nap - Lets Mossy nap.

!giftpebbles - Gift your collected pebbles to another user.

!pebbles - Shows how many shiny pebbles you've collected.

!mossybag - Displays your Mossy inventory and statistics.

!mossyboard - View Mossy's pebble leaderboard.

💚 Friendship Commands

!petmossy - Pet Mossy.

!talk - Chat with Mossy.

!pets - Shows your friendship with Mossy.

!level - Shows your current level and XP.

!mossyprune - Resets your level and XP.

🐟 AFK System

"Mossy brb" - Sets your AFK status.

"Mossy I'll brb" - Sets your AFK status.

"Mossy I'll be right back" - Sets your AFK status.

"Mossy set my AFK" - Sets your AFK status.

Mossy automatically:

• Remembers your AFK reason.

• Tracks how long you've been away.

• Tells others you're AFK if they mention you.

• Welcomes you back when you return.

• Remembers your AFK status even if Mossy restarts.

Tip: You can add a reason after your AFK message.

Example: "Mossy brb eating dinner"
