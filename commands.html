🐟 Mossy Commands

Everything Mossy can currently do!

💬 Basic Commands

!hello - Mossy says hello!

!moss - Mossy reacts based on his current mood.

!think - Random fishy thoughts.

!mood - Shows Mossy's current mood.

!changemood - Gives Mossy a new mood.

🫧 Fun Commands

!bubble - Creates bubbles.

!feed - Feed Mossy.

!nap - Lets Mossy nap.

💚 Friendship Commands

!petmossy - Pet Mossy.

!talk - Chat with Mossy.

!pets - Shows your friendship.

!level - Shows your current level and XP.

🐟 Automatic Triggers

"mossy" - Mossy might randomly reply.

"hi mossy" - Mossy gets excited.

"hello mossy" - Friendly greeting.

"hey mossy" - Friendly greeting.

"hai mossy" - Friendly greeting.

Rowan saying "I'm alive" - Mossy celebrates Rowan returning.
