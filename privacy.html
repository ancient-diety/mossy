🔒 Mossy's Privacy Policy

Mossy believes shiny pebbles should be collected... not personal information. 🐟

💙 What information does Mossy collect?

Mossy only stores the information needed to remember your progress.

  • Discord User ID
  • XP
  • Level
  • Friendship
  • Pets
  • Pebbles
  • Pebble statistics (given and received)

🐟 Why is this stored?

Mossy uses this information so your progress isn't lost every time the bot restarts. Without it, your friendship, levels, pets and pebbles would reset.

🔒 What Mossy does NOT collect

  • Passwords
  • Email addresses
  • Payment information
  • Your Discord messages
  • Your real name
  • Your IP address

🌐 Website

Mossy's website does not require an account or login. It is simply an information page and does not intentionally collect personal information from visitors.

🗑️ Want your data removed?

If you'd like your saved Mossy data deleted, you can contact the bot owner, ancient_diety on discord, and it can be removed from the bot's save file.

💭 Where is user info stored?

User ID's and memory are all stored in a JSON file and are not shared or distributed. Info is kept safe and private.
